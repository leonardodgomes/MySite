The University Center of FEI, São Bernanrdo do Campo - Brazil

Graduation Project: In the ceramic industries area, the quality analysis of the tile pieces was not done satisfactorily. The project had the main goal, automate this process, making it more practical and reliable. For this, we use pictures of ceramic tiles, taken before they went through the painting phase. The resulting image is then sent to the analysis system, which detects defects such as cracks, edge deformities and invalid measurements. We use equalization to change the lighting of images if necessary, detect edges to detect flaws, morphology and math to highlight more discrete defects. After all defect analyzes, a classification is made, to separate the parts that will be sold and the parts that will be discarded. The analyzed tiles were compared with a user- defined model as an ideal piece. At the end of the classification, the system issues a report with data analysis of the parts.
